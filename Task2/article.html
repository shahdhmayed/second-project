Animals

Animals are living creatures that can move, eat, and sense the world around them. They live in many different places animals, Animals are important to the Earth. They help balance nature and keep the environment healthy. It’s important for people to protect animals and their habitats so they can live and thrive. Animals are multicellular, living organisms that: Breathe oxygen, Consume food (they're heterotrophs), Reproduce (sexually or asexually), Move (at least at some stage of life), Have specialized cells and organs. They’re found everywhere — deep oceans, high mountains, forests, deserts, even inside other organisms.
