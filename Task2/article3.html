Coffee

Coffee is a popular drink made from roasted coffee beans. It contains caffeine, which helps people feel more awake and alert. Enjoyed hot or cold, coffee is a daily habit for millions around the world People enjoy it in many forms, like espresso, latte, or iced coffee. Coffee al so brings people together in cafés and social setting. Origins and History: Legendary beginnings: According to legend, coffee was discovered by an Ethiopian goat herder named Kaldi, who noticed his goats became energetic after eating red berries from a certain tree. Spread through the Arab world: Coffee was first brewed in Yemen in the 15th century, then spread through the Islamic world. Europe & beyond: By the 17th century, coffeehouses became popular in Europe, often called “penny universities” for the intellectual discussions they encouraged. Colonial cultivation: European colonial powers introduced coffee to the Americas and Southeast Asia.
