Water

Water is essential for all living things, helping plants grow and animals survive. It covers about 70% of the Earth’s surface and is found in rivers, lakes, and oceans. Water is the source of life, keeping our bodies and the environment healthy. It is used for drinking, cooking, cleaning, and growing food. Importance of Water 🌱 In Nature & Life: Essential for living organisms: Used in digestion, transport of nutrients, waste removal. Photosynthesis: Plants use water to produce oxygen and glucose. Habitat: Oceans, lakes, rivers, wetlands—home to countless species. Weather & Climate: Drives the water cycle (evaporation → condensation → precipitation). 🏠 In Daily Life: Drinking, cooking, cleaning, bathing Agriculture (irrigation) Industry (cooling, manufacturing) Power generation (hydropower)
