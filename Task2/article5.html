Red flower

Flowers are beautiful parts of plants that come in many colors and shapes. They attract bees, butterflies, and other insects for pollination. Flowers are used to express feelings, decorate spaces, and celebrate events. Each type of flower has its own meaning and unique fragrance. Classification of Flowers Based on symmetry: Actinomorphic (radial): e.g., rose, sunflower Zygomorphic (bilateral): e.g., orchid, snapdragon Based on plant type: Annuals: complete life cycle in one season (e.g., marigold) Perennials: live and bloom for several years (e.g., tulip) Biennials: life cycle in two years (e.g., foxglove)
